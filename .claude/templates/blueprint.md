# Template: Blueprint

> A Blueprint is a non-authoritative planning artifact that precedes and
> informs a future Domain Architecture document
> (`docs/00-governance/xp-0010-documentation-writing-standard.md` §3). It
> carries no authority of its own, is not held to the Domain Architecture
> pattern, and never satisfies the Approval Gate for anything. Place it
> under the relevant domain's `blueprints/` folder (see
> `docs/02-core-platform/blueprints/` for the existing convention).
