# 00 — Company

**Status:** Foundation Draft

## Purpose

This folder holds the foundational identity documents of XETROIT as a
company and platform — the "why" behind everything else in this
documentation set. It exists to give every audience (investors, developers,
architects, and AI agents) a shared understanding of intent before they
encounter any technical detail.

## Documents That Belong Here

- Company vision, mission, and philosophy
- Long-term strategic direction and guiding principles
- Values and decision-making principles that inform architecture and
  product trade-offs
- Non-technical context an investor would need to understand XETROIT's
  purpose

Documents that describe *what the platform does technically* belong in
`01-platform/` or later folders, not here. This folder is strictly about
identity, vision, and philosophy.
