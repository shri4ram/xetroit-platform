# 03 — Business OS

**Status:** Foundation Draft

## Purpose

This folder documents the Business OS module — the set of features and
services that let organizations operate their business functions on the
XETROIT Platform.

## Documents That Belong Here

- Business OS feature scope and architecture
- Domain models specific to business operations (e.g. organizations,
  teams, workflows, business-facing tooling)
- Business rules governing this module's behavior
- Integration points between Business OS and the core platform or other
  modules

Cross-module or platform-wide concerns belong in `01-platform/` or
`02-core-platform/`, not here.
